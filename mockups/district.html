Strengths
  • Largest toor dal production hub in India — 75 active dal mills
  • 2.5–4 billion MT proven limestone reserves (200-400 year supply)
  • Strategic NH-50 and rail connectivity to Hyderabad & Bangalore
  • PM MITRA textile park approved (₹390 Cr, Dec 2024)
⚠️
Challenges
  • Lowest per capita income in Karnataka (₹1.46L vs state avg ₹2.42L)
  • Only 13.4% irrigated land — 86.6% rainfed agriculture
  • 43% of Bangalore construction workers are migrants from Kalaburagi
  • Credit-Deposit ratio at 69% (target: 85%+)

Opportunities
  • Garment & textile cluster: 26,000 direct jobs, ₹1,800 Cr revenue potential
  • Aerospace components — 200km from Hyderabad aerospace hub
  • Pharma packaging — linked to Hyderabad Pharma City Phase 2
  • Dal mill upgradation: 500 mills targeted by 2032 (currently 75)
